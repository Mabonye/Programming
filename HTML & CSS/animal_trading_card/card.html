Lion

Female lions do most of the hunting, while males are responsible for protecting the pride.

  • Scietific Name: Panthera leo
  • Average Length: 1.4-2 metres
  • Average Lifespan: 10-15 years
  • Habitat: Savannah, scrub, grasslands and some wooded areas

Lions have strong, compact bodies and powerful forelegs, teeth and jaws for puling down and killing prey. Their coats are yellow-gold and adult males have shaggy manes that range in color fromm blond to reddish-brown to black.
